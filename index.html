Types of hackers

White Hat Hacker

A white hat is an ethical computer hacker.

Grey Hat Hacker

A grey hat is a computer hacker or computer security expert who may sometimes violate laws or typical ethical standards.

Black Hat Hacker

Black Hat hackers are criminals who break into computer networks with malicious intent.
